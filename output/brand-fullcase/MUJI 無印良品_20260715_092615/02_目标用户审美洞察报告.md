User Aesthetic Insight Report

**Objective:** To analyze the core target user group’s visual aesthetic preferences and psychological drivers.

**Key Insights:**
1. **Texture Preferences:** High-end users prefer rich, tactile textures like velvet and polished metals, while comfort-oriented users favor soft, natural textures such as cotton and wood.
2. **Color Atmosphere:** Luxury is associated with deep, rich colors and metallic accents, whereas minimalism is linked to neutral palettes that create a calming environment.
3. **Space Sense:** High-end users value spaciousness and open layouts, while minimalist users prioritize functional space without excess.

**Conclusion:** Understanding these preferences allows brands to tailor their product designs to align with user expectations, enhancing brand appeal and user satisfaction.
