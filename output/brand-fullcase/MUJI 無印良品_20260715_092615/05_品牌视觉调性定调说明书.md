Brand Final Tone Positioning Specification

**Objective:** To define the brand’s tone and positioning in the market based on research insights.

**Tone Definition:**
- **Sophisticated Minimalism:** Emphasizing elegance and simplicity in communication and design.
- **Eco-Conscious Innovation:** Highlighting sustainable practices and innovative features.
- **Timeless Nostalgia:** Creating a sense of timelessness through retro-inspired elements.

**Conclusion:** This tone should guide all brand communications and design decisions to ensure consistency and alignment with consumer expectations.
