Product Visual Gene Report

**Objective:** To identify the visual elements that define the brand’s product identity and differentiate it in the market.

**Key Elements:**
1. **Signature Minimalism:** Incorporating clean lines and simple forms as a core design principle.
2. **Eco-Friendly Materials:** Utilizing sustainable materials to reinforce the brand’s commitment to environmental responsibility.
3. **Nostalgic Touches:** Integrating retro elements to evoke a sense of nostalgia and uniqueness.

**Conclusion:** These visual genes should be consistently applied across product lines to strengthen brand identity and recognition.
