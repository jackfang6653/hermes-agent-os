Full-Link Design Traceability Manual

**Objective:** To document the design process and ensure traceability from concept to final product.

**Process Documentation:**
1. **Concept Development:** Initial ideation based on market and user insights.
2. **Design Iteration:** Refinement of designs to align with brand visual genes and user preferences.
3. **Finalization:** Approval and production of designs that meet brand standards.

**Conclusion:** This manual ensures transparency and consistency in the design process, facilitating future iterations and innovations.
